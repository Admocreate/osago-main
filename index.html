Who We Are

We are a transportation and logistics company that leverages technology to connect people and businesses with boats. The OsaGo Ride app was created to allow you to book a boat from over 40 jetties across Lagos at your leisure. Our mission is to reduce traffic congestion in Lagos and assist commuters in getting to their destinations quickly and safely while enjoying the beauty and tranquillity of the Lagos waterways.
